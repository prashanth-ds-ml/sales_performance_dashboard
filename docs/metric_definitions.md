# Metric Definitions

| Metric | Formula | Business Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Total Sales | Sum of `sales` | Revenue generated |
| Total Profit | Sum of `profit` | Profitability after sales and discount effects |
| Total Orders | Count of order rows | Order-line volume |
| Average Discount | Average of `discount` | Discounting intensity |
| Profit Margin % | Profit / Sales | Profitability rate |
| MoM Revenue Growth % | Current month sales vs prior month sales | Monthly revenue movement |
| Category Sales Share % | Category sales / total sales | Category contribution |
| Region Sales Share % | Region sales / total sales | Regional contribution |
| Average Ship Days | Ship date - order date | Fulfillment speed proxy |
